🌍 Topic: Climate Change and Its Impact on Our Planet
Introduction
Climate change refers to the long-term changes in temperature and weather patterns on Earth. In recent years, climate change has become a serious global issue mainly due to human activities.
Causes of Climate Change
The main causes include:
Burning of fossil fuels like coal and petrol, which release harmful gases
Deforestation, where trees are cut down and cannot absorb carbon dioxide
Industrial pollution and vehicle emissions
These activities increase greenhouse gases, leading to global warming.
Effects of Climate Change
Climate change has many harmful effects:
Rising temperatures across the world
Melting glaciers and increasing sea levels
Extreme weather like floods, droughts, and cyclones
Loss of biodiversity and harm to animals and plants
Solutions to Climate Change and
We can reduce climate change by:
Using renewable energy like solar and wind power
Planting more trees
Reducing plastic use and waste
Saving electricity and water
Spreading awareness among people
Conclusion
Climate change is a major threat to our planet, but it can be controlled if we take action now. Every individual has a role to play in protecting the Earth for future generations.
